I'm currently a principal researcher at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University in 2015 and B.S. degrees in Software Engineering from Beihang University in 2010. I was as a visiting student in UCLA from September 2013 to September 2014. In 2015, I joined Networking Research Group at MSRA.

Please feel free to send me an email if you are interested in full-time research position, internship, or joint ph.D program.

My research interests are in the broad areas of systems and networking. My recent work is mainly focusing on:

  • AI Infrastructure and Machine Learning Systems

  • AI for Systems and Networking

  • Cloud Disaggregation
